## LEGAL BASIS

This request is made under the **Washington Public Records Act (RCW 42.56)**, which provides a strong presumption of public access to government records.

Key Washington laws governing surveillance:
- **RCW 42.56** — Public Records Act (broad right of access)
- **RCW 9.73** — Privacy Act (warrant requirements for electronic surveillance)
- **RCW 10.93.160** — Shield Law (prohibits immigration enforcement cooperation)
- **SB 6280 (2020)** — Facial Recognition Accountability Law
- **Rodriguez v. City of Sedro Woolley (2025)** — Court confirmed surveillance data is public record
